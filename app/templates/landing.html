Swiss AI is envisioned to be a personal AI-centric multitool, drawing inspiration from the versatility of Swiss Army Knives. My goal is to offer users (and myself) a straightforward, web-based dashboard that grants access to a suite of developer tools powered by OpenAI—and potentially other providers in the future—without the need for programming expertise. By prioritizing user-friendliness, I aim to ensure that all users, regardless of their technical background, can enjoy the full spectrum of benefits, including customization and advanced features typically reserved for non-web applications.

Currently, I'm working to integrate an array of functionalities, such as GPT Conversations, GPT-Vision, Text-to-Speech (TTS), Embeddings/Knowledge Retrieval, and Transcription/Translation powered by Whisper. The platform is designed with a simple profile system to preserve user preferences and manage authorizations efficiently. The Sign-Up and Login pages are readily accessible below.

To follow the development progress and access the source code, visit the project's GitHub page here.
