Embeddings

  • Embeddings are high-dimensional numerical vectors that represent the semantic properties of words or phrases.
  • Text is divided into 'tokens,' which can be words, parts of words, or punctuation, serving as the input units for AI processing.
  • Users can define 'chunk size' to determine how text is segmented, directly impacting the AI's reference scope during Q&A sessions.
  • Customizable Title and Author fields enable the AI to provide precise document references, enhancing the relevance of AI responses.
